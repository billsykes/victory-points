When teams are tied in total Victory Points, the following tiebreaker hierarchy is used (following Yahoo Fantasy Football standards):

1
Total Victory Points

Primary ranking criteria. Each team can earn 0-2 Victory Points per week (1 for head-to-head win + 1 for performance win).

2
Total Fantasy Points

Sum of all weekly fantasy points scored throughout the season.

3
Most Recent Week Score

Fantasy points scored in the most recent completed week.

4
Second Most Recent Week Score

Fantasy points scored in the second most recent completed week.

5
Continue Back Through All Weeks

Third most recent week, fourth most recent week, and so on through all completed weeks.
